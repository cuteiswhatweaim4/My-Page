Quantum Computing

Quantum computing is a type of computing that uses quantum bits, or qubits, which can exist in multiple states simultaneously. This allows quantum computers to process information in ways that classical computers cannot.

Blockchain Technology

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across many computers. It ensures that the recorded transactions cannot be altered retroactively, which adds a layer of security.

Augmented Reality

Augmented reality (AR) overlays digital information onto the real world, enhancing the user's experience. It has applications in gaming, education, and various industries.
